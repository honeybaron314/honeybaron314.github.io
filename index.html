About Us

We believe nature has created a perfect product for your mind and body and need not be messed with.

We produce organic, unfiltered, never-heated, untreated and in any way unadulterated honey from our local bees.

We are based in Stamping Ground, KY and our honey is made from our own fields and orchards, untreated with any chemicals or pesticides.

Our Goal: Pure Honey from the Bees to You!

Portfolio

Kentucky has a wonderfull climate and flora allowing us to create three types of honey.

Spring honey is made from late April through early June containing primarily black locust, cherry, apple, pear (from our orchard) and dutch clover nectar from our fields. Spring honey is lite in color with very sweet and mild flavor.

Fall honey is made from mid June through early August containing primarily poplar and dutch clover. Fall honey is darker/amber in color with much less sweet yet more spicy/cinamony flavor.

Winter honey is made from early September through late October containing primarily aster and golden rod nectar. Winter honey is the darkest in color but we let our bees keep this honey for the winter.
